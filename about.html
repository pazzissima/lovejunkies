Love Junkies The Musical

Premiering at the American Conservatory Theater A.C.T.

Costume Shop Theater in San Francisco

October 3-5th, 2014

Love Junkies is a broadway style original musical celebrating Love in many of its aspects, playful, romantic, intimate, erotic, seductive, fatherly, friendly, committed, uncommitted.

Love Junkies characters crave it, chase it, discover it, cope with the lack or loss of it in their own way, some are able to channel it into love for something else.

Loss and tragedy spin the Love Junkies into their "dark night of the soul" experience that allows them to get in touch with their shadow parts, and also bring up a sense of compassion for themselves and others, even in unexpected moments and circumstances.

The Love as Life Force wants to live and move forwards even in Love Junkies.
